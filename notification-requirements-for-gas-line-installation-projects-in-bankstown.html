Protocols for leak incidents

Gas line installation and repair are essential infrastructural projects in Bankstown, ensuring the smooth operation of natural gas services to residents. However, in the rare occurrence of a leak incident, stringent protocols must be in place to address and rectify the situation promptly. Immediate detection and response to gas leaks are crucial in safeguarding public safety and minimizing potential risks to the community.

Upon detection of a gas leak, the first step is to evacuate the area and contact the local gas service provider as well as emergency services. It is imperative to avoid any open flames, smoking, or operation of electrical appliances in the vicinity of the leak to prevent potential ignition. Subsequently, qualified personnel equipped with the necessary safety gear should assess the situation, isolate the leaking section, and proceed with repairs following established safety protocols.
