Emergency ShutOff Procedures

In the event of an emergency, understanding the proper shut-off procedures for gas lines is crucial to ensure the safety of individuals and property. Gas line installation and repair personnel must be well-versed in the steps to swiftly and effectively shut off the gas supply in case of a leak or other hazardous situation. Additionally, property owners should also familiarise themselves with these procedures to take immediate action before professional help arrives.

The first step in shutting off a gas line during an emergency is to locate the main shut-off valve. The valve is typically located near the gas meter and may require the use of a wrench to turn it. Once the valve is located, turn it clockwise to shut off the gas flow. It is essential to practice and be comfortable with this procedure beforehand to avoid any delays or confusion during a crisis. Remember, safety is paramount in all situations concerning gas line installation and repair.
