Bankstown Bites Food Festival

Bankstown Bites Food Festival is an iconic event that showcases the diverse culinary delights of Bankstown and its surrounding areas. The festival offers a vibrant food experience, featuring a wide range of cuisines from various cultures. Visitors have the opportunity to indulge in delicious dishes, from traditional favourites to modern fusion creations.

Aside from the scrumptious food offerings, Bankstown Bites Food Festival also hosts entertaining performances, cooking demonstrations, and fun activities for all ages. This community event brings people together to celebrate food, culture, and creativity. Whether you're a food enthusiast or simply looking for a day of enjoyment, the festival promises a memorable and flavourful experience for everyone.

Paul Keating Park

Nestled in the heart of Bankstown, Paul Keating Park is a picturesque green space that offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. The park boasts lush greenery, beautiful flower beds, and plenty of open spaces for picnics or leisurely strolls. Visitors can bask in the tranquillity of the park while enjoying a book, having a relaxing picnic with family and friends, or simply unwinding amidst nature's beauty.

For those seeking a bit of adventure, Paul Keating Park features a playground area that is perfect for children to let off some steam and have a fun-filled day out. The playground is equipped with swings, slides, climbing frames, and more, ensuring that kids of all ages can enjoy a day of outdoor play in a safe and engaging environment. Whether you're looking to relax in nature or entertain the little ones, Paul Keating Park offers something for everyone to enjoy in Bankstown.

Paul Keating Park



Bankstown City Gardens

Bankstown City Gardens is a serene and lush oasis nestled in the heart of Mount Lewis. The garden boasts a diverse range of plants and flowers that captivate visitors with their vibrant colors and soothing fragrances. Strolling through the well-manicured paths, visitors can admire the beauty of native Australian flora and exotic plant species carefully curated to thrive in the local climate.

The Gardens provide the perfect setting for a leisurely picnic or a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life. Families, couples, and solo visitors alike can find solace in the tranquil surroundings while enjoying a relaxing day outdoors. With its picturesque landscapes and inviting atmosphere, Bankstown City Gardens is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and anyone seeking a tranquil escape in Mount Lewis.

Bankstown City Gardens



Bankstown Library and Knowledge Centre

Situated in the heart of Bankstown, the Bankstown Library and Knowledge Centre is a hub of learning and community engagement. Boasting a vast collection of books, magazines, and digital resources, the library caters to the diverse interests and needs of its visitors. The comfortable reading areas and conducive study spaces make it a popular spot for students and book lovers alike.

In addition to its extensive collection, the Bankstown Library and Knowledge Centre offers a range of programs and activities for all ages. From storytelling sessions for children to technology workshops for adults, there is always something exciting happening at the library. Whether you're looking to expand your knowledge, participate in a community event, or simply enjoy a quiet afternoon with a good book, this vibrant cultural institution has something for everyone.
