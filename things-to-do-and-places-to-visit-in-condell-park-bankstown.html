Bankstown City Gardens

Bankstown City Gardens is a peaceful haven located in the heart of Condell Park. This lush green space offers visitors a chance to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse themselves in nature. The gardens boast a variety of native Australian plants, beautifully manicured lawns, and winding pathways perfect for leisurely strolls.

Visitors can take a moment to relax by the tranquil pond, which is home to ducks and other wildlife. There are also designated picnic areas where families and friends can enjoy a meal amidst the picturesque surroundings. Bankstown City Gardens is not only a place for relaxation but also a great spot for birdwatching, with a range of bird species frequenting the area.

Bankstown City Gardens



The Australian Aviation Museum

The Australian Aviation Museum in Bankstown is a must-visit for aviation enthusiasts and history buffs alike. Located within the Bankstown Airport precinct, the museum showcases a rich collection of aircraft and aviation artifacts that highlight the significant role Bankstown has played in Australia's aviation history. Visitors can explore a range of aircraft, from vintage planes to modern jets, and learn about the pioneering pilots and engineers who have shaped the nation's aviation industry.

One of the highlights of the Australian Aviation Museum is the opportunity to see up close iconic aircraft like the Spitfire, Canberra bomber, and Mirage jet. The museum's knowledgeable staff are on hand to provide insights into the history of each aircraft and share fascinating stories about their role in Australia's aviation heritage. Whether you're a professional aviator, a history enthusiast, or simply curious about the world of flight, a visit to the Australian Aviation Museum offers a captivating journey through Australia's aviation evolution.

The Australian Aviation Museum



McLeod Reserve

McLeod Reserve in Condell Park, Bankstown is a hidden gem worth exploring for nature enthusiasts and families alike. The reserve boasts lush greenery, tranquil walking paths, and vibrant birdlife, providing the perfect setting for a leisurely day outdoors. Visitors can take a leisurely stroll along the winding paths, have a picnic by the picturesque pond, or simply relax and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere that McLeod Reserve offers.

The reserve also features modern playground facilities, making it a popular spot for families with young children. Kids can run around and play on the swings, slides, and climbing frames while parents can unwind on the nearby benches and soak in the serenity of the surroundings. McLeod Reserve is a wonderful destination to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse oneself in nature's beauty right in the heart of Bankstown.

McLeod Reserve



Bankstown Bites Food Festival

Bankstown Bites Food Festival is a vibrant celebration of the diverse cuisines and rich cultural heritage that make up the vibrant community of Condell Park. This annual event draws food lovers from near and far to sample an array of delicious dishes from local vendors, showcasing the multicultural flavors of the area. With culinary delights ranging from traditional Lebanese kebabs to mouth-watering Vietnamese pho, there is something to satisfy every palate at this exciting festival.

In addition to the delectable food offerings, Bankstown Bites Food Festival also features live music performances, cooking demonstrations, and a bustling market selling artisanal products and fresh produce. Attendees can immerse themselves in the lively atmosphere, soaking up the sights, sounds, and smells of this gastronomic extravaganza. Whether you're a devoted foodie looking to expand your culinary horizons or simply seeking a fun day out with family and friends, Bankstown Bites Food Festival is a must-visit event that encapsulates the vibrant spirit of Condell Park.
