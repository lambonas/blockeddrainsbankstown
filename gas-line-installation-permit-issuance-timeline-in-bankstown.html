Renewal Application Requirements

For the renewal of a gas line installation permit in Bankstown, strict requirements must be met to ensure continued compliance with safety regulations. When applying for a renewal, the applicant is required to submit an updated application form providing details of the proposed gas line installation or repair. This information should include the exact location of the project, the type of gas being used, and the materials to be utilised in the installation or repair process.

Moreover, along with the application form, the applicant must provide detailed plans and drawings of the proposed gas line installation or repair. These plans should clearly showcase the layout of the gas line, indicating the depth at which it will be installed, its distance from other underground utilities, and any safety measures to be implemented during the installation process.
