FAQS

Can backflow be completely prevented?

Yes, the only 100% way to prevent backflow is by installing and maintaining a backflow prevention device in your plumbing system.

What is the purpose of a backflow prevention device?

A backflow prevention device is designed to stop contaminated water from flowing back into the clean water supply, ensuring the safety of your drinking water.

How often should a backflow prevention device be tested?

Backflow prevention devices should be tested annually by a certified backflow prevention specialist to ensure they are functioning correctly and providing maximum protection.

Are there different types of backflow prevention devices for residential and commercial properties?

Yes, backflow prevention devices can be tailored to suit the specific needs of both residential and commercial properties, ensuring effective protection against backflow in any setting.

Why is it important to hire a certified backflow prevention specialist for backflow prevention?

Certified backflow prevention specialists have the knowledge and experience to assess your plumbing system, recommend the right backflow prevention device, and ensure it is installed and maintained correctly for optimal protection.
