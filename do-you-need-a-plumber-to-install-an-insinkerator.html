Maintenance Advice

For individuals considering garbage disposal installation and repair, proper maintenance is essential in ensuring the longevity and efficiency of the InSinkErator system. Regular maintenance tasks include checking for leaks, ensuring all connections are secure, and inspecting the blades for any signs of wear or damage. It is recommended to run cold water while using the garbage disposal to flush out debris and prevent blockages that could hinder its functionality.

In addition to regular checks, it is advisable to refrain from putting certain items down the disposal unit such as fibrous foods, fats, grease, and other items that may cause clogs or damage to the system.
