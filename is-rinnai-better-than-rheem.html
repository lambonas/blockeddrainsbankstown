Maintenance Requirements

Maintenance requirements are essential considerations when deciding between Rinnai and Rheem water heaters. Both brands require regular upkeep to ensure optimal performance and longevity. Water heater installation and repair professionals recommend periodic inspections to check for sediment buildup, leaks, or other issues that may affect the unit's efficiency.

Regular maintenance tasks include flushing the tank to remove sediment, checking the pressure relief valve, and inspecting the pilot light for proper functioning. Professional servicing is also advised to address any potential issues before they escalate into costly repairs. Keeping up with the maintenance schedule not only prolongs the lifespan of the water heater but also ensures consistent hot water supply for your household.
