Bankstown City Gardens

Located in the heart of Bankstown, the City Gardens offer a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of urban life. As you step into the gardens, you are greeted by a fragrant bouquet of native Australian flora, including vibrant banksias, waratahs, and kangaroo paws. The well-manicured lawns provide a perfect spot for a leisurely picnic with family and friends, while the well-maintained pathways are ideal for a relaxing stroll amongst the greenery.

The City Gardens also feature a charming duck pond, where you can watch the ducks swim gracefully in the serene waters or feed them breadcrumbs for a delightful experience. Be sure to keep an eye out for the array of bird species that call the gardens home, from colourful lorikeets to majestic cockatoos. Whether you're looking for a tranquil spot to unwind or a picturesque setting for a morning walk, the Bankstown City Gardens offer a delightful escape into nature right in the heart of the city.

Bankstown Arts Centre

Bankstown Arts Centre provides a vibrant hub for creative expression in the heart of Bankstown. The centre showcases the rich cultural diversity of the community through a wide range of exhibitions, workshops, and performances. Visitors can immerse themselves in the local arts scene by exploring the various galleries and attending events that celebrate the talents of both established and emerging artists.

The Arts Centre also offers educational programs for individuals of all ages to discover and enhance their artistic skills. From painting and sculpture classes to dance and theatre workshops, there is something for everyone to unleash their creativity. Whether you are a seasoned artist or just starting out, the Bankstown Arts Centre is a welcoming space where you can be inspired and connect with like-minded individuals who share a passion for the arts.

Bankstown Arts Centre



Bankstown Bites Food Festival

The Bankstown Bites Food Festival is a celebration of the diverse culinary landscape that Bankstown has to offer. It is a vibrant event that showcases a wide array of cuisines from different cultures, reflecting the multicultural fabric of the area. Visitors can indulge in a feast for the senses, sampling delicious dishes from local restaurants, food trucks, and market stalls.

The festival is not just about food; it also features live music, cultural performances, and entertainment for all ages. It is a fantastic opportunity for the community to come together, enjoy good food, and celebrate the rich cultural tapestry of Bankstown. Whether you are a foodie looking to try something new or simply wanting to immerse yourself in the lively atmosphere, the Bankstown Bites Food Festival is a must-visit event for locals and visitors alike.

Bankstown Bites Food Festival



Bankstown Markets

Bankstown Markets is a vibrant and bustling hub that offers a wide range of goods and products for visitors to explore. From fresh produce to unique handmade crafts, there is something for everyone at this lively marketplace. The inviting atmosphere of Bankstown Markets makes it the perfect place to spend a leisurely weekend morning, browsing the stalls and enjoying the local offerings.

With a diverse array of vendors showcasing their wares, Bankstown Markets provide an opportunity to support small businesses and discover hidden gems. Whether you're in search of fresh flowers, tasty treats, or one-of-a-kind gifts, you're sure to find something special at this beloved community event. The friendly faces and lively chatter add to the charm of Bankstown Markets, making it a must-visit destination for locals and tourists alike.
